About Us

The ACM Student Chapter at South Asian University is a vibrant community of students passionate about computing, innovation, and collaboration. We are dedicated to creating a space where ideas thrive, projects take flight, and learning never stops.

Through workshops, hackathons, expert lectures, and community-driven projects, we aim to foster technical skills and leadership among students. Join us to be part of an international network and shape the future of tech.
